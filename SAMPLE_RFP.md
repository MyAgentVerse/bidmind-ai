# REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
## Enterprise Cloud Migration Services

**Issued by:** Global Tech Solutions Inc.
**Date:** January 15, 2024
**Proposal Due Date:** February 15, 2024
**Decision Date:** March 1, 2024

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Global Tech Solutions Inc. is seeking a qualified vendor to support the migration and modernization of our enterprise IT infrastructure to cloud-based solutions. We currently operate on-premise data centers with legacy applications and require a comprehensive cloud transformation strategy.

The scope includes infrastructure assessment, cloud platform selection, migration planning, and execution support with minimal business disruption.

---

## 2. BUSINESS BACKGROUND

**Organization:** Global Tech Solutions Inc.
**Industry:** Financial Services & Technology
**Current State:**
- 500+ employees across 3 locations
- Legacy on-premise infrastructure (15+ years old)
- 50+ applications (mix of custom and commercial)
- 10TB+ of data requiring migration
- Annual IT budget: $2.5M

**Business Drivers:**
- Reduce capital expenditure on infrastructure
- Improve system reliability and uptime (current SLA: 99.2%, target: 99.95%)
- Enable faster deployment of new applications
- Improve disaster recovery capabilities
- Modernize technology stack

---

## 3. SCOPE OF WORK

The vendor shall provide the following services:

### Phase 1: Assessment & Planning (Months 1-2)
- Current infrastructure audit
- Application portfolio analysis
- Cloud readiness assessment
- Risk assessment
- Migration roadmap development

### Phase 2: Infrastructure Design (Month 2-3)
- Cloud architecture design (multi-cloud or single cloud)
- Security and compliance design
- Network design and connectivity
- Disaster recovery strategy

### Phase 3: Migration Execution (Months 4-8)
- Infrastructure provisioning in cloud
- Application migration (prioritized waves)
- Data migration and validation
- Testing and UAT support
- Cutover and go-live management

### Phase 4: Optimization & Support (Months 9-12)
- Performance optimization
- Cost optimization
- Knowledge transfer and training
- 90-day post-implementation support

---

## 4. MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS

**Vendors must meet ALL of the following to be considered:**

1. **ISO 27001 Certification** - Information Security Management
2. **Cloud Platform Experience** - Minimum 5 years with AWS, Azure, or GCP
3. **Financial Services Experience** - At least 3 completed projects in financial services industry
4. **Dedicated Account Manager** - Full-time primary contact throughout engagement
5. **24/7 Support Availability** - Support team available 24 hours, 7 days a week
6. **SLA Guarantee** - Minimum 99.9% uptime guarantee
7. **Performance Bond** - Performance bond equal to 10% of contract value
8. **Team Certifications** - At least 10 cloud architects with advanced certifications (AWS Solutions Architect, Azure Solutions Architect, or equivalent)

---

## 5. EVALUATION CRITERIA

Proposals will be evaluated on the following criteria:

| Criteria | Weight |
|----------|--------|
| Technical Capability & Approach | 40% |
| Cost & Commercial Terms | 30% |
| Experience & References | 20% |
| Team & Staffing | 10% |

### Technical Capability (40%)
- Clarity and feasibility of proposed approach
- Innovation in cloud strategy
- Phased migration plan clarity
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Disaster recovery and business continuity approach

### Cost (30%)
- Overall project cost (fixed vs. time & materials)
- Cost breakdown by phase
- Value for money
- Payment terms

### Experience (20%)
- Number of similar projects completed
- Project success rate
- Reference quality
- Industry-specific experience

### Team (10%)
- Team composition and qualifications
- Relevant experience of key personnel
- Availability and commitment

## 8. SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS & CONSTRAINTS

**Technical Requirements:**
- Multi-region deployment capability
- Support for both Windows and Linux environments
- Database migration support (SQL Server, Oracle, PostgreSQL)
- On-premise to cloud hybrid connectivity for transition period
- Compliance with financial services regulations (SOC 2 Type II, PCI DSS)

**Timeline Constraints:**
- Cannot migrate during Q2 (business-critical quarter)
- Minimal downtime required (maximum 4-hour window per application)
- Phased migration approach mandatory

**Personnel Constraints:**
- Vendor must not replace key personnel without approval
- Minimum 2 full-time on-site resources for 12 months
- Weekly status meetings with executive sponsor

---

## 9. RISKS & CONCERNS

The following risks have been identified:

- **Data Loss Risk** - Large data volumes require careful migration planning
- **Application Compatibility** - Some legacy applications may not be cloud-compatible
- **Regulatory Compliance** - Financial data handling requires strict compliance
- **Business Continuity** - Any extended downtime impacts customer services
- **Knowledge Gap** - Internal IT team limited cloud expertise
